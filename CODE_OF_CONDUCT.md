# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

BuildTwin Vision welcomes constructive, respectful collaboration. Participants are expected to be clear, professional, and considerate when discussing issues, pull requests, design tradeoffs, and review feedback.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, publishing private information, or knowingly introducing unsafe instructions, secrets, or misleading claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not meet this standard. Repeated or severe violations may result in restricted participation.

## Reporting

If you need to report conduct concerns, use a private maintainer contact path when one is available. Do not disclose private personal information in public issues.
