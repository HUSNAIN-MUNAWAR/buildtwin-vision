1. IfcOpenShell geometry/property adapter and web IFC viewer.
2. Camera calibration and semi-automatic BIM registration.
3. Celery/Dramatiq worker with Redis, retries, cancellation, and job leasing.
4. S3-compatible immutable evidence storage and retention policies.
5. Construction-specific ONNX detector adapters with dataset/model cards.
6. Full CPM calendars, float, constraints, and P6/MS Project integration adapters.
7. Contractor velocity history and calibrated probabilistic delay models.
8. Webhook/email/Teams/Slack delivery with signing and retry history.
9. Enterprise SSO, project-level memberships, granular permissions, and compliance exports.
10. Playwright end-to-end CI and visual regression baselines.
